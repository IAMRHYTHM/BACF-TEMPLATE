BALAJI AGRO CHEMICALS AND FERTILIZERS

Established in the year 2010 we, ”balaji agro chemicals & fertilizers” are distinguished manufacturers of micro-nutrient fertilizer mixture and supplier of agricultural products and chemical fertilizers. Manufactured using the latest scientific techniques, these products have a made a name for themselves owing to their unmatched quality, high efficacy and environment friendliness. Not only do they help increase the productivity of the soil, but are also fit for repeated use due to their environment friendly nature. They do not have any toxic properties, which effectively mean that the consumables grown using these fertilizers will not have any harmful pathogen. In fact they provide the plants with essential nutrients which help them grow healthier and quickly. These fertilizers are mostly used in the agriculture sector for cultivation of crops and plants. Our fertilizers eliminate all the harmful microorganisms while maintaining the original composition of water. we aim to be a one stop solution for all agriculture related needs of our clients. Therefore, we have built a cutting-edge manufacturing facility which helps us produce our full product portfolio simultaneously with amazing ease. The quality of the raw material used is unquestionable, since we source them only from the authorized and reputed vendors. Our devoted team of professionals, with assistance of the hi-tech machines, develops a qualitative range of products which conforms to various international standards. Owing to our efforts in maintaining highest standards of quality, we achieved iso 9001:2008 certification. we have an able guide and mentor in “mr. M. K. Tyagi”, who has a valuable treasure of vast industrial experience and deep product knowledge. His never-say-die attitude motivates each worker to give his best and climb up the ladders of success.
